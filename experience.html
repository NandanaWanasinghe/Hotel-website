EXPERIENCES

Create unforgettable memories with an abundance of eco-friendly and sustainable experiences at Amaya Resorts and Spas. We believe in creating moments that are unique and jaw-dropping that goes above the norm of your regular guest experience. Prepare for some serious #travelgoals with us.

BEE HARVESTING

With the maintenance of bee colonies in and around the resort, guests can now experience the taste of home-harvested honey and also help build a more sustainable world. Amaya Hills, Kandy is part of a growing collection of bee-friendly landmarks across the island, an initiative the resort takes pride in, given the documented decline in the bee population in certain parts of the country.
Producing local grown honey, the team at Amaya Hills also teaches their guests about the importance of these insects as well as pollination of greenery throughout the city, thus raising awareness by a hosting thriving colonies of bees.

THE AMUNA EXPERIENCE

The perfect place to rediscover yourself with this holistic healing science at Amuna. Located just a two-minute drive away from Amaya Lake, Dambulla, Amuna is a place of pure tranquility, designed to blend seamlessly with nature and is surrounded by lush forests overlooking the Kandalama lake.
Amuna Ayurveda Retreat fits the definition of wellness for any type of guest. Here, wellness goes beyond massages and herbal baths, and helps you make cultural connections through yoga, meditation and authentic Sri Lankan cuisine.

JET SKIING

Spend an afternoon on the water with a range of beach activities. Pasikudah is a global sensation for the adventure lovers. The calm seas make it even better and is ideal for skiers of all age groups and expert levels.
Let your hair down and relax and enjoy water skiing at its best.

BANANA BOAT

Cue fits of laughter and exciting screams as you enjoy a ride on a banana boat as it skims and breaks through sea waters while being pulled by a speedboat!

CORAL REPLANTING

A unique experience where the team at Kuda Rah will transplant your frame and let you monitor its progress. During your next visit, a snorkeling adventure awaits you as you can view the coral frame you planted.

DIVING

One of the finest diving spots in Kuda Rah, observe enchanting creatures of the deep from grey reef sharks to Napoleon fish and sea turtles. A wealth of marine diversity is ready to greet you at Kuda Rah. With warm clear waters, the Maldives is a diver’s paradise. No matter what your diving passion is, you are sure to find it here in Kuda Rah.

KAYAKING

Paddle away and discover the wonders of the world around you and explore the ocean from a different vantage point.
Spend your days paddling across the beautiful ocean whilst enjoying unparalleled views with un spoilt clear waters whether you’re a beginner or advanced kayaker.

STAND UP PADDLE BOARDING

As the golden sun bronzes your shoulders and the crisp, salty breeze flows around you, enjoy a stand up paddle boarding experience in these tropical, beautiful ocean waters.
Look around to enjoy the scenic landscape of both land and sea, or look down for a crystal-clear close-up of the ocean ‘aquarium’ beneath you,. Stand up paddle boarding is also an excellent way of maintain your cardio quota!
